🧘‍♀️ Mindful Moments - Site Plan

Site Name

Mindful Moments – This name reflects a digital space where users can take short, meaningful breaks to breathe, reflect, and reset through guided mindfulness activities. It’s calm, inviting, and focuses on individual well-being.

Optional domain availability: mindfulmoments.app

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide a simple and guided experience for users seeking quick mindfulness practices. It includes breathing exercises, reflection prompts, and gratitude listing activities to help users unwind, reduce stress, and practice self-awareness.

Scenarios

  • “I only have five minutes. What can I do to feel more relaxed and centered?”
  • “How can I guide myself through a reflection activity to feel more grounded?”

Color Schema

Typography

  • Raleway (Sans-serif) – Used for headings and titles to give a modern, clean feel.
  • Open Sans (Sans-serif) – Used for body text for readability and simplicity.
